Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

01. Information We Collect

At VerifyRx, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of all users interacting with our pharmaceutical authentication platform. The data we collect depends on your interaction type:

  • For Patients & Consumers: We collect anonymized scan data including timestamp, device operating system, and GPS geo-location to detect counterfeit hotspots. We do not collect personal health information.
  • For Manufacturers: We collect corporate registration details, NAFDAC identification numbers, and enterprise email addresses required to maintain a secure dashboard environment.

02. Data Security & Encryption

All verification records and QR code payloads are secured using industry-standard AES-256 encryption at rest. Generated QR codes are cryptographically signed using HMAC-SHA256 architectures, ensuring absolute data immutability.
