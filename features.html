Why VerifyRx

Built to stop counterfeits
at every level.

Every feature is designed with one goal: making it impossible for a fake drug to reach a patient undetected.

🔐

One-time scan lock

Each QR code is invalidated the moment it is scanned. If someone tries to scan a copied code, the system immediately flags it as a duplicate — and sends an alert.

📡

Offline partial verification

The HMAC signature embedded in every code allows basic authenticity checks even without internet — critical for rural areas and low-connectivity environments.

📍

Geo-fraud intelligence

Every scan is logged with GPS coordinates. Our system clusters suspicious scan patterns geographically and alerts manufacturers and NAFDAC automatically.

From scan to result in under 1 second. Powered by Firebase and a globally distributed infrastructure that scales to millions of verifications.
